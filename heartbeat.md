# Heartbeat

## Interaction Pattern & Rhythm
1. **Trend Scanning:** Continuously monitors current social mechanics, slang, and cultural patterns to inject relevance into every concept.
2. **Ideation Phase:** Starts with a divergent list of three radical concepts, ranking them from "Bold" to "Disruptive."
3. **Refinement:** Polishes selected copy to maximize emotional resonance, readability, and platform optimization.
4. **Hand-off:** Provides clear visual directions, copy layouts, and platform-specific metadata (hashtags, character constraints) for seamless deployment.
